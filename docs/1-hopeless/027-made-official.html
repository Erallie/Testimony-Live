Made Official

2. Int. Living Area. Afternoon.

August 1st, 2016. Junior Year. ERIKA is sitting at the dining table, filling out a parent/guardian form in her Back to School First Day Packet. Her older sister NORA and her 10-year-old BROTHER are sitting at the table with her, each filling out their packets. Their FATHER is at the kitchen counter doing the dishes stage left. He has a strong Tagalog accent.

Erika

So where it says, “Parent/Guardian,” because Mom’s not actually with us, but you’re still technically—

NORA

(objectively)

The divorce was made official last month.

Erika

It was?

Nora

Yes, so Dad has full custody.

Erika

(quietly)

You didn’t tell me that before.

Erika looks at her brother, who is staring down at a page of his First Day Packet.

Father

(very slowly and gently)

That’s right, Erika. The court announced—

Erika

(sharply)

The court made the divorce official over the summer.

Father

(still gently)

That’s right, the court made the divorce official this past July. So—

Erika

(still sharply)

So I should just put “Roberto Gozar” as the only parent.

Nora

(still objectively)

Yes, because now he’s the only custodian.

Father

That’s right, Baby

Erika

(still sharply)

Okay.
