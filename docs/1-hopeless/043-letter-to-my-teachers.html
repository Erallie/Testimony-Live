Many a time over the break, it seemed that though the ideas that came were not remaining in my head in English words, I swiftly got some kinds of ideas for fractions of a second as I read the paper (as I naturally should). They did not remain in my head with the same intensity or clarity as when I had received them because more thoughts were rushing in to harass me and blocking them out. All I needed to do to write the paper was to force myself to ignore the wary, stalling questions that were always in the back of my mind and to put my pencil on the paper and write. I simply could not convince myself to do that.
