She Hit Me

3. Int. Erika’s Bedroom. 7:46 pm.

Fall 2016. ERIKA is kneeling silently in her closet doorway in indecision, head bowed, her eyes on the ground below her. She has been kneeling there for at least an hour or two. Her sister NORA, who is one year older than she is, opens her bedroom door and stands in the doorway.

Nora

(with callous authority)

Erika. Get up.

Erika remains unresponsive, observing Nora warily from her peripheral vision. A few seconds pass.

Nora (Cont’d)

Erika.

Erika blinks but still doesn’t speak. Nora enters the bedroom and approaches Erika. Erika’s chest tightens.

Nora (Cont’d)

Get up.

Erika remains silent.

Nora (Cont’d)

(tugging her arm abrasively)

Get up, Erika.

Erika doesn’t move.

Nora (Cont’d)

(hitting her in the arm)

Come on Erika, get up!

Erika flinches but otherwise remains motionless.

Nora (Cont’d)

(hitting her again, more aggressively)

Get up!

Erika flinches again but otherwise doesn’t move. Nora grabs Erika’s arm and grunts as she drags her through the closet door. Erika lets out a muted whine as she’s dragged across the floor. A couple feet into the bedroom, Nora loses her grip. Nora grunts louder as she falters. She steadies her feet and turns again to Erika.

Nora (Cont’d)

(shouting aggressively)

Get up Erika!!

Nora grabs Erika’s arm and pulls at it again. Erika pushes her feet on the ground against Nora’s tugging. Their FATHER approaches the bedroom door and stands in the doorway. He has a strong Tagalog accent.

Nora (Cont’d)

(shouting)

GET UP!!

Father

(angrily)

What’s all the shouting about?!

Nora

Erika won’t get up!

Erika

Nora hit me! Twice!

Father

(to Nora, yelling incredulously)

Don’t hit her!

Nora

But she won’t move!

Father

(yelling incredulously)

So you don’t hit her! You stand there and tell her to get up! You don’t hit her!

The father pulls the back of his shirt up his neck with a single sharp sweep of his hands behind his shoulders.

Father (Cont’d)

(still yelling incredulously)

And if she doesn’t move, okay then! You just walk away!

Nora

But—

Father

(still yelling)

—You don’t hit her!

There is silence for a good five seconds.

Father (Cont’d)

(peeved)

Go to your room!

After a beat, Nora lets go of Erika’s arm and walks out the bedroom door as their father steps back to make room for her. The father huffs peeved as he turns back to the hallway he came from.

Father (Cont’d)

(huffing angrily as he walks away)

Pu-tang ina!
