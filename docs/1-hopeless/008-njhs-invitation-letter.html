(NJHS Invitation Letter)

Thursday, August 14, 2014

Dear Erika Gozar:

Congratulations! Based on your cumulative GPA, you have been invited to apply for membership in the BASIS Peoria chapter of the National Junior Honor Society. Membership in the NJHS is the highest honor given to a junior high student and carries with it a distinction recognized nation-wide. We encourage you to apply for this exceptional opportunity. As you complete your application, please keep in mind the following requirements must be met:

  • You must have a cumulative grade point average of at least a 3.50 out of 4.0 for the Upper School and a 90.0 out of 100 for Lower School following the third quarter marking period.
  • You must complete the NJHS application including the required essay and obtain the required signatures.
  • You must be an active member in at least two extracurricular activities. One of the two may be from participation in a recent outside organization (with a qualifying signature on the application).
  • A minimum of one activity must be from BASIS Peoria during the current school year.
  • Students must exemplify good citizenship and character. You must have a clean discipline record.
  • Students are expected to be outstanding in the area of scholarship, service, character, leadership, and citizenship throughout the school year. All five areas are of equal importance in determining membership.
  • Once accepted, members will be expected to maintain the qualification standards including a grade point average of at least 3.25 out of 4.0 for the Upper School and 87.0 out of 100 for Lower School. Students will also be expected to complete 30 hours of community service throughout the school year (20 hours in/for the BASIS Peoria school building and 10 hours in the community).

Throughout the next school year, NJHS will meet monthly and participate in various service opportunities. If eligible, students will be expected to serve as peer tutors with a one-hour-per-week peer tutoring requirement. Peer tutoring hours will count towards the service requirement within the building. Your membership experience in NJHS will create unique opportunities for service, leadership, and character that you will carry with you throughout the rest of your life. Please submit the completed application to Ms. Nadir in Room 7 by 4:00 p.m. on Friday, August 22nd. We will not accept late applications. Your application and essay will be reviewed by the NJHS Faculty Council that consists of five teachers. You will be informed of your acceptance into NJHS by the end of the school day on Tuesday, August 26th. If you have any further questions, please contact Ms. Nadir.

Respectfully,
