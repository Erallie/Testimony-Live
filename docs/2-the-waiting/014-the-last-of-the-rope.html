The Last of the Rope

June 11, 2024

I will try with my everything,
If you choose to throw me
The last of the rope I’ve already wasted.

I will grab hold of it,
And I will climb,
With the last of the strength that you gave me in the past.

And although the line is already tight
From the weight of my failure pulling it down,
I will hold the rope close to my chest,
And I will climb,
And I will not let go,
Until maybe… one day
I can reach you at the top.

But I will not give up,
Until I’m there with you.
If you only throw it down
One final time.
