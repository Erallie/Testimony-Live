Okay so I started praying in the middle of my last entry, and I ended up crying. I'm gonna cut ahead.

I was thinking about suicide this morning. I don't currently remember the thoughts, but I remember an angel pointing it out to me when I was more awake after a couple of hours lying on my bed, and I also remember remembering it after they pointed it out to me.

When I went out to the kitchen later, another voice told me that I wasn't thinking about suicide, and I said they were right—I wasn't thinking about killing myself: I was thinking about the practicalities of if I were to kill myself, which I know I was doing after I had gotten out of bed. But later on, when I was back in my room, the angel reminded me again that they told me I was thinking about suicide, and that was while I was still on my bed: I was probably thinking about both.

I was being mean to myself this morning.

There's a lot to write about that I don't really want to put the effort in to write about.

I haven't read the Bible for the last three days.

Zoe said they'd just finish Dead on the Vine by themselves. (I did ruin their birthday part 2 for them.)

At least I got the rating done yesterday. The rating is nice.

I don't feel like going into the bad stuff that happened yesterday, but I regressed a lot in maturity as a result. I think.
