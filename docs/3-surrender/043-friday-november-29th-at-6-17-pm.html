Friday, November 29th at 6:17 PM

So good stuff happened. Last night, I stopped writing that entry because I was thinking about stuff, and I started crying. And I ended up praying that God do what he wants to do in my heart and in my life, because I can't do things myself, so I need Him to do it for me. And I think there was some type of surrender or something in it, and I know I've prayed that God do what He wants to do in my heart and life before, but I didn't pray for him to do the things I cannot do. And that's what I prayed this time. And I think God heard, because I felt (and still feel) that he is there.

But there was an emotional barrier between me and Him. And when God asked if my previous love/passion for Him was dead, I said it was probably hidden behind dissociation and doused in tears. And I remembered the theory I had of me potentially having felt like God abandoned me. And I realized that might have been the cause for the separation or barrier.

So things are good. I remembered things, and God is here. And now, you know, they just need to be resolved.

Because if I was able to reconnect with God's love again by rectifying with myself the feeling of God having abandoned me, I wouldn't need another person to provide me that deep emotional support I crave because I would be able to go to God for that. And of course, I should still seek other people to connect with, because the Bible says not to forsake the gathering of God's elect, but I wouldn't be so broken and alone all the time, and I would know that God is there.

So things are good. And God is there. I just need to trust that God will resolve the thing with me feeling like He abandoned me.

I don't understand why sometimes I have to ask before it happens. I figure that some of these things, like God working in my heart and life, are just promises in the Bible, and I figured they would happen anyway. I don't understand why I needed to pray specifically for God to come through where I fail and overcome my shortcomings for him to do stuff in my life again.
