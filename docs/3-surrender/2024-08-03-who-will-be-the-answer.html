Saturday, August 3rd at 4:21 PM

I'm trying to think of ways to make my life more meaningful. I still haven't worked on the Ark, because other things with the note-taking app Obsidian got in the way, and I started making plugins with it.

A couple days ago, it seemed like God or an angel was telling me that Garret was ready and was preparing me for him to come back the next day. But I was denying everything, like I was used to doing with Chase. First, I told God that I wasn't asking for him not to come back tomorrow, but for God to use His discretion, since I was responding the way I was, which might signify that I wasn't ready. But then I kept complaining, and after a lot of it, it felt like I had pushed God not to answer. But after that and into the next couple of days, I was asking God that if Garret was meant to be an answer, that he'd come back and be an answer (since I previously stated that he had never yet been able to). And then the following days, I asked a similar thing, that he would come back, but that God would remain my focal point.

I should just trust that God will answer the prayer of my heart when I first prayed that prayer months ago. And that he would answer all of my prayers throughout the years regarding it. That whoever it would be, they'd direct me to God over themselves, that they would lead me and guide me toward God, that I could share my life with them, and we'd love each other and both love God with all our hearts. And God would be the center point of our lives.

God was my everything before. He gave me meaning, and because of Him life was more than worth it. I don't remember it now. But I reason that he is a better fit for my hole than any human being can be. So I reason that that's what I should pursue.

And God gave me the answers as to how to pursue Him. If we love God, he would come and make His stay with us. And if we love God, we would do His commands, and His command is to love others as He has loved us. So all I need to do, is love. And he will come and make His stay with me.

I wrote about it in my notes. Let me copy and paste it here:

  • I want to know God, be close to Him, and excel in my faith
    • To do this, I need to love others:
      • ““If you love Me, you shall guard My commands.”” (John 14:15 TS2009)
      • ““He who possesses My commands and guards them, it is he who loves Me. And he who loves Me shall be loved by My Father, and I shall love him and manifest Myself to him.”” (John 14:21 TS2009)
      • ישוע answered him, “If anyone loves Me, he shall guard My Word. And My Father shall love him, and We shall come to him and make Our stay with him.” (John 14:23 TS2009)
      • ““As the Father has loved Me, I have also loved you. Stay in My love. If you guard My commands, you shall stay in My love even as I have guarded My Father’s commands and stay in His love.””(John 15:9-10 TS2009)
      • ““This is My command, that you love one another, as I have loved you. No one has greater love than this: that one should lay down his life for his friends.”” (John 15:12-13 TS2009)
    • If I love others, God will reveal himself to me. And that is how I seek God.

But it's difficult sometimes to love when I'm not being reminded to do so when I can. Over the past couple of days, I've been working on plugins and ignoring Dad when he told me to do stuff, which isn't love.

It's difficult on my own. And the Bible said that the Holy Spirit would remind us. But it doesn't always seem to happen. It seems to depend on my state of mind.

I sort of asked God maybe yesterday for someone to act as a guide. But like… I'd think God would be enough…?

I think maybe this is why it says in the Bible to encourage one another and build each other up, and not to forsake the assembling of God's elect.
