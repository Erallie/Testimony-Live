But that will be later. There are other things I have to write about.

I learned yesterday that I had picked up complaining to a very large degree. I thought it was probably from Garret, but it seemed to only get worse after the thing with Nora and Sam happened and I thought Garret was (or wasn't) coming back.

I wrote yesterday in my notes that it's difficult to love others when you don't already feel loved. So I have to choose whether to find a source of love from elsewhere, or power through long enough for God to show me His love. I will copy and paste what I wrote here:

  • It's difficult to love others when you don't feel or believe that you are loved. Therefore, it's difficult if you don't already feel loved to love others so that God will reveal His love to you.
    • Do I find a source of love first?
    • Or do I push through to try to love first?
      • Will God still be my source of strength?
      • Or is that source of strength His love which I do not have?

I asked God to give me understanding of this, and He told me to write it down, so I did.

Another thing, I had a dream last night involving someone who looked like Damian from BASIS but I understood to be Garret. I thought it was Garret because there was already another person there who looked like Damian, and I thought that there can't be more than one Damian. So the other person has to be someone else.

I asked them who they were, and they responded in a way that indirectly stated that I don't see them for who they are, or something else which I understood to mean that I'm not seeing them as the right person.

So I called them by the name Garret, and they responded, “Yes?” And I said, “I knew it was you,” relieved.

This was in a high school setting. And I also volunteered to go up to the front and improv some singing with lyrics. The teacher asked, “Are you sure you can do it?” And I said I know I can improv singing, but I'm more iffy on improv'ing lyrics. But I wanted to know if I could do it.

So I sang in front of the class, and while I sang, everyone started raising their hands up and forming a thumbs down in a very weird and slow way. I had no idea why everyone was doing that.

After I finished, the teacher said that it looked like everyone was dissatisfied with it, as though I was being judged for an evaluation. And I thought unhappily, “I didn't know I was being judged!” And she called on one of the students to share their thoughts, and they said that I just can't sing.

And something about the way she responded (or maybe the way everyone in the class was responding) made me feel attacked or something because I got up and physically grabbed and pinched her while saying that I am not horrible at singing, that I am good at singing, and that it's just that I'm not so good at improv.

And while I did that, a thought was telling me that Garret was there watching, and I said, “I don't care.”

And I squeezed her until a voice said that I am squeezing her spirit out of existence, and then I eased up a bit.

I thought I'd write that part too because it was also on my mind, and I was thinking about both parts of the dream while I was working today.
